About diyaMD

DiyaMD is a software application with many unique capabilities that improve a medical practice's revenue, efficiency, and delivery of health care. Patients' records are kept up-to-date and accessed easily. It was developed by Diya Health, a team of doctors and engineers, and is being used on four continents with most users in North America.
